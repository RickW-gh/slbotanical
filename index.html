About Botanical

Botanical was created in 2007 in the online virtual world of Second Life.

Since 2007, Botanical has won multiple community awards for it’s products in Second Life.

Botanical started as way to share 3D environment assets made for personal use. As a brand, Botanical quickly grew in it's first year to become a permanent virtual landscaping fixture in Second Life.

Straylight, the region that hosts the original Botanical store, has been featured in the Second Life Destination Guide Editor’s Picks and has served as a stage for several community created machinimas as well as a major network tv show.

Kriss Lehmann, (the inworld avatar for Botanical's creator) was the focus of the first episode of the “World Makers” documentary series about Second Life content creators.

What We Make

Nature and environment assets for virtual worlds.

From plants, trees, rocks and terrain textures to lighting, wall, fences and path kits. We provide a wide range of assets to enhance your virtual world.

In addition, we provide dynamic features and systems from seasonal environment changes to dynamic weather and audio effects.
